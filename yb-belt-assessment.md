# Yellow Belt Assessment

## Yellow Belt Assessment @showdialog 
To show you are ready for Orange Belt, complete the Yellow Belt Mastery Mission to demonstrate your understanding of the concepts taught in Yellow Belt, including loops, conditionals, variables, events, and sequencing.

Create a project that fulfills all of the requirements on the left side. Click Next and alert a Code Sensei when you are ready to have your project checked.

Click **Ok** to get started!

## Requirements

A complete Belt Assessment must meet the following requirements: 

- :paper plane: Use at least 3 different **sprite kinds**, including a Player sprite.
- :paper plane: Use **velocity** to make sprites move or adjust the speed at which a sprite **follows** another sprite.
- :paper plane: Use at least 2 **overlap functions** to make something happen when a Player sprite overlaps a different sprite kind. 
- :game controller: Use an **event** to control a sprite with **direction buttons** or make something happen when the **A button is pressed**.
- :id card: Use **variables** to set and change the Player's score, lives, and/or start a countdown timer.
- :repeat: Use at least 1 **loop** to make sprites or projectiles repeatedly appear on screen.
- :shuffle: Use at least 2 **conditionals** to make something happen when something is true. Use “else” for at least 1 of the conditionals.
- :calculator: Use **random numbers** and **chance** to change when and how sprites appear, where they are positioned, or how fast they move.
- :paint brush: Add a **background color**, **background image**, or **screen effect**.
- :paint brush: Add a **sprite effect** or **sprite animation** to the project.
- :keyboard: Use on-screen text with a **splash screen**, **dialog box**, or **sprite text**.
- :music note: Add **sound effects** or a **looping melody** to the project.
