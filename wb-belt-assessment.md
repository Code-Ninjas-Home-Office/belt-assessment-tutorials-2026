# White Belt Assessment

## White Belt Assessment @showdialog 
To show you are ready for Yellow Belt, complete the final Prove Yourself of White Belt to demonstrate your understanding of the concepts taught in White Belt, including creating sprites, sprite movement, and events.

Create a project that fulfills all of the requirements on the left side. Click Next and alert a Code Sensei when you are ready to have your project checked.

Click **Ok** to get started! 

![Logo](https://github.com/Code-Ninjas-Home-Office/game-building-session-tutorials-2024/blob/master/images/CN-Logo.png?raw=true "CN Logo") 

## Requirements

A complete Belt Assessment must meet the following requirements: 

- :paper plane: Create a **Player sprite** and set it to **move** with the direction buttons and to **stay in screen**.
- :paper plane: Create an **Enemy sprite** and set its position away from the Player sprite. Set it to **follow** the Player sprite, and adjust the follow **speed**.
- :paper plane: Create at least 3 sprites for the Player sprite to search inside. Set 1 as a **Goal sprite** and the others as **Food sprites**, and set their **position** to different locations on screen.
- :paper plane: Use 2 **on sprite overlaps sprite** container blocks to **win** the game when the Player overlaps the Goal, and **lose** the game when the Player overlaps the Enemy.
- :paper plane: Use an **on sprite overlaps sprite** container to **change the Food sprite image** using the **otherSprite** bubble when the Player overlaps a Food sprite.
